Understanding Security Controls

Published by Cris • 2026

Security controls are safeguards or countermeasures designed to protect information systems from threats and risks. They play a critical role in ensuring confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data.

As part of my cybersecurity training, I conducted a control and compliance audit for a fictional organization called Botium Toys. This exercise helped me understand how security controls align with compliance requirements and risk management.

Through this audit, I learned how organizations identify gaps in their security posture and implement controls to reduce exposure to threats. These lessons are essential for anyone starting out in cybersecurity.
